NUNA Branding

NUNA Logo

Our logo identifies, expresses and distinguishes us. It’s one of the first things people see and remember when they think about us. It’s what enables us to stand out from the rest.

When we introduced the new logo we wanted to make sure it accurately represents the essence of NUNA, showing the world our boldness and dynamism.

The eye is among the most recognizable symbols in the world, the circle around the eye is inspired to Vitruvian Man of Leonardo da Vinci. With our logo we created an image that is instantly recognizable in a way that identifies the project.
